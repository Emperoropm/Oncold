ABOUT
US

ONCOLD is more than streetwear. It's a mindset.


Founded in 2022, ONCOLD was built for the ones who don't wait, don't settle, and don't follow. It represents a generation driven by hunger, discipline, and vision. Our philosophy is simple: Wake. Hustle. Slay.


Built for those who don't follow standards.

They create them.

OUR
HISTORY

2022
Founded

ONCOLD was established in Nepal with a group of friends driven by a hustler mindset. What started as an idea turned into a movement built on ambition, discipline, and the drive to create something bigger.

2024–25
Building Identity

Developed signature designs like Real Eyes and Medusa, shaping the brand's bold and meaningful aesthetic.

2026
Expansion to Toronto

ONCOLD steps into the global scene, growing its presence in Toronto, Canada.
